Everything you need

Built for the real boutique

Not some generic POS slapped with a logo. Every feature was designed around how clothes shops actually work.

Fast Sales
Add items, select size and colour, choose Cash, M-Pesa or Credit — done in seconds. Your cashier will love you for it.
Size & Colour Variants
Every product tracks its own sizes and colours. No more selling a Medium when you only have Larges left.
Live Dashboard
Revenue charts, top products, payment method breakdown, and date filtering. Know exactly how your business is doing, right now.
Supply Management
Add suppliers, raise purchase orders, and receive stock. When stock arrives, inventory updates automatically.
Credit Tracking
Record credit sales with customer names and phone numbers. Mark balances as paid when they settle. No more chasing ghosts.
Staff & PIN Access
Each employee gets a 4-digit PIN. Cashiers see only what they need. Managers get more. Owners get everything.
Profit Margins
Enter buying and selling prices. Manguo calculates your margin automatically — so you know what's actually worth stocking.
5 Elegant Themes
Champagne, Midnight, Sage, Blush, or Slate. Switch themes in one click. Because even software should look good.
Your Data, Only Yours
Every business owner's data is completely isolated. Your stock, sales, and staff are invisible to everyone else on the platform.
